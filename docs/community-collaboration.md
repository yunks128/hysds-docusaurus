# Open Source Community and Collaboration

HySDS thrives on contributions from a wide community of developers and mission teams. **33 NASA-funded projects** and **13 active projects** are using HySDS as of 2024.

## Community Involvement:

- **GitHub**: [HySDS GitHub](https://github.com/hysds)
- **Jira and Confluence**: Tools for coordination and feature requests.
- **Slack Channels**: Active channels for collaboration across projects.
  
## Recent Contributions

- Integration of real-time analytics.
- Multi-mission coordination meetings held bi-weekly.
